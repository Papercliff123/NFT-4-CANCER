Radical Transparency

The blockchain creates an immutable ledger of donation. We partner with regulated medical institutions to ensure every ETH spent translates to patient care.

Audited by CertiK (Example)

1. Primary Sale

When you mint an NFT, 90% of the funds are automatically routed to a Multi-Sig Wallet managed by our board and institutional reps.

2. Institution Deployment

Funds are converted to stablecoins and transferred directly to verified wallet addresses of partner hospitals. No middlemen.

3. Patient Impact

Partners issue quarterly on-chain reports detailing treatments funded. The NFT metadata updates to reflect the lives impacted.
